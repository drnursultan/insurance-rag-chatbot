Insurance Billing and Payments

You can pay your insurance bill online, by phone, or through automatic withdrawal from your bank account.

Late payments may result in a temporary policy suspension, so it’s important to pay on time. Most insurers offer a 7–10 day grace period before cancellation.

To set up autopay:
1. Sign in to your online account.
2. Go to Billing → Payment Options.
3. Choose automatic payments and select your preferred method.

If you need help with billing issues, contact our support team:
- Billing Hotline: 1-800-555-0199 (Mon–Fri, 8 AM–6 PM)
- Email: billing@yourinsureco.com

Refunds for overpayments are automatically credited to your next statement. Paperless billing is available for all policyholders.
